Mulching

Mulching is the practice of covering the soil with a layer of material to suppress weeds, improve soil quality, and conserve moisture.

03

Plowing

Plowing is the process of turning over the soil to prepare it for planting. It is one of the oldest agricultural practices, and it is still used today on farms all over the world.

04

Mowing

Mowing is the process of cutting grass or other vegetation using a lawnmower. It is a common practice in agriculture, landscaping, and homeowners' yards.

05

Seeding

Seeding is the process of planting seeds in the soil. It is an essential part of agriculture, and it is used to grow a wide variety of crops, including food, feed, and fiber.
