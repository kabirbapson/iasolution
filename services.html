Key Components of the Offerings

Offerings Services
Farm Discovery Farm discovery uses a technology-driven approach to farmer and farm onboarding with processes for the following:
  • KYC/AML,
  • Geospatial detailing for farm mapping, identification of tube wells, and open water sources to mitigate drought spells due to climate change.
Community Discovery The Community Management Module provides communities with the ability to manage services like EOP Management, Input Management (Distribution), Recovery Management, Extension Service Management, Storage
Agent Discovery The Agent Discovery Module provides agents with the ability to avail themselves as a key touchpoint for service delivery of services listed under service provisioning
Service Provisioning Service Provisioning Module provides service providers with the tools to onboard their products and services for farming communities like:
  • Farmer onboarding (KYC/AML)
  • Farm Mapping (geospatial detailing)
  • Input distribution (stock-in, stock-out)
  • Extension service/Monitoring & Evaluation
  • Crop protection services (aerial applications of chemicals, seeds, and fertilizer)
  • Animal protection services (identification, breading, and disease control)
  • Mechanization/Tractorization
  • Recovery services
  • Logistics/Haulage
  • Storage
Banking as a Service Wallet System (Bank account, and Grain account integration)
  • Payment enabled.
  • Trading enabled (linked wallet with stored inventory value)
Marketplace Integration Off-taking (crop off-taking, animal off-taking)
  • Crop Off-taking (Grains and oil seeds)
  • Animal Off-taking (Meat and Hide/Skin)
