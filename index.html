Innovative AgriSolution (IAS) - Farm Cycle Digitisation for Smallholder Farmers

More than 1 billion individuals are actively engaged in small-scale agriculture,
a collective effort responsible for producing 70% of the world's food supply.

Our mission is to establish connections and provide essential support to these farmers, empowering them to enhance their productivity and reshape global supply chains to their advantage.

Our vision is to unlock the economic potential of 10 million small-scale African farmers by the year 2033.
